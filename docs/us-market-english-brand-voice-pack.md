# 美国市场英文品牌语气与单品页文案方向

> 对应任务：[HTH-273](/HTH/issues/HTH-273)
> 适用范围：首页 Hero、notify-only PDP、FAQ、邮件收集区、后续 brand voice guide
> 当前前提：`preview-only + notify-only`，不开放价格、库存、交期、检测结论或购买承诺
> 更新时间：2026-04-21

## 一句话判断

美国市场这版英文不该写成“东方器物故事”或“资料博客说明文”，而要写成 `edited, product-first, low-hype launch copy`:

- 先说这是什么，再说为什么值得看。
- 用具体名词，不用空泛形容词。
- 可以讲 form、material、 silhouette、setup presence。
- 不提前讲性能、工艺神话、检测结论或交易承诺。
- 语气要像 `design-minded product launch page`，不是 `research diary`。

## 当前可借鉴的英文语气模式

2026-04-21 查看美国站官方页面后，可归纳出 3 个稳定模式：

- Hario USA：产品名先行，紧接一段短说明，强调 shape、pour control、materials、capacity 这类可验证信息。
- Fellow：headline 更利落，先给一句 product promise，再拆成 highlights / overview / details，语气直接，不绕品牌故事。
- KINTO USA：允许更有审美感，但依然落回 form、comfort、texture、control 这些具体体验词，不靠夸张奢侈词撑气氛。

对本店最合适的组合不是照搬任何一家，而是：

- 用 KINTO 式的 calm, object-led tone。
- 保留 Fellow 式的 short headline + structured details。
- 维持 Hario 式的规格和材质清晰度。

不建议走的方向：

- 过度“匠人叙事”
- 过度“东方禅意”
- 过度“精品咖啡性能承诺”
- 过度“奢侈品腔调”

## 英文总语气规则

### 推荐写法

- Short sentence first.
- Concrete noun before abstract mood.
- Product before brand myth.
- Visual character before performance claim.
- Explicit boundaries before conversion ask.

### 句式建议

- `Copper pour-over kettle`
- `Bottle-shaped silhouette`
- `Copper body`
- `Non-silver-lined version`
- `Catalog-listed specs, pending sample review`
- `Product preview`
- `Notify-only for now`

### 不建议的句式

- `Oriental craftsmanship`
- `Ancient hand-made tradition`
- `Luxury artisanal heritage`
- `Perfect flow control`
- `Food-safe certified`
- `Premium purple copper`
- `Arrival notice`
- `Reserve yours now`

## 一句话英文定位：3 个方向

### Direction A: Calm object-led

`Thoughtful copper objects for slower, more intentional brewing.`

适合：最稳妥，未来可扩到茶具、咖具、餐具，不局限单一 kettle。

### Direction B: Category-expansion friendly

`Copperware for coffee, tea, and the table, edited down to what matters.`

适合：既保留店铺品类扩展空间，又不把首页写成 broad lifestyle brand。

### Direction C: Launch-page friendly

`Thoughtful copper objects, introduced one piece at a time.`

适合：当前单 SKU preview 阶段，能自然衔接“现在先看这一件”。

### 推荐优先级

推荐先用 `Direction C` 做当前阶段对外定位，再在样品验证和品牌扩品后切到 `Direction B`。

## Hero headline + subheadline：3 个方向

首页首屏建议遵循这个结构：

- Headline: 只负责建立产品记忆点
- Subheadline: 补版本、状态、边界
- Body: 再补 notify-only 说明

### Direction 1: Product-first

- Headline: `A Copper Pour-Over Kettle With a Bottle-Shaped Silhouette`
- Subheadline: `Copper body, non-silver-lined version, and a product preview built around clearly labeled details.`

适合：最接近当前站点需要，先把“是什么”说清楚。

### Direction 2: More editorial

- Headline: `One Copper Kettle, Shown With Clear Boundaries`
- Subheadline: `This preview focuses on the non-silver-lined version and catalog-listed specs while sample details are still under review.`

适合：当前 `preview-only` 语义最强，能避免首页像缺价格的销售页。

### Direction 3: More launch-like

- Headline: `A Copper Brew Object Worth Following`
- Subheadline: `Bottle-shaped, copper-bodied, and currently available as a notify-only preview while final details are being reviewed.`

适合：更像单品发布页，但仍保留边界感。

## Hero body 文案写法

Hero body 不要写长。保持 1 到 2 句，总共 24 到 40 个英文词为宜。

### 推荐模板 1

`This page is a product preview for the non-silver-lined version. Pricing, availability, and sample-verified details will only be added after review.`

### 推荐模板 2

`Current details are limited to verified or clearly labeled catalog information. For now, the only live action is product updates.`

### 推荐模板 3

`We are showing this piece with clear boundaries: object, form, and listed specs first; pricing and launch details later.`

## CTA 语气原则与可直接使用的短句

### 原则

- CTA 只做 `low-commitment update capture`，不做成交暗示。
- Primary CTA 用第一人称更自然：`Notify Me`。
- Secondary CTA 只引导看详情，不引导排队、预约、锁名额。
- CTA 附近必须补一句 boundary copy，防止被误读成预售。

### 推荐主按钮

- `Notify Me`
- `Get Product Updates`
- `Stay Updated`

### 推荐次按钮

- `See Product Details`
- `Read the Notes`
- `Check the Specs`
- `Why Preview Only`

### 推荐辅助说明

- `For product updates only. No reservation or purchase commitment.`
- `This sign-up is for updates only. Pricing and availability are not live yet.`
- `You are signing up for product updates, not a preorder or waitlist.`

### CTA 绝对不要写

- `Reserve Yours`
- `Join the Waitlist`
- `Pre-Order Now`
- `Claim Your Spot`
- `Get Early Access`
- `Shop Now`
- `Buy Now`

## PDP 关键模块的英文表达原则

目标不是把 PDP 写成性能页，而是写成 `clear, design-forward product preview page`。

### 1. Material

应该怎么写：

- `Copper body`
- `Non-silver-lined version`
- `Catalog-listed lid and handle materials`

怎么写更自然：

- 先写用户能理解的主体材质，再补待复核字段。
- 优先用 `copper`，不强行翻成 `purple copper`。

不要怎么写：

- `purple copper`
- `pure copper` unless verified
- `silver inner wall`
- `food-safe copper`

### 2. Craft / form

应该怎么写：

- `Bottle-shaped silhouette`
- `Curved profile`
- `Visually balanced form`
- `Details will be confirmed after sample review`

怎么写更自然：

- 可以谈 shape、proportion、surface feel、object presence。
- 不要把“工艺感”写成空泛大词，要落到可见形态。

不要怎么写：

- `master craftsmanship`
- `ancient technique`
- `hand-forged` unless verified
- `heirloom quality` unless supported

### 3. Use case

应该怎么写：

- `For pour-over setups`
- `Designed for a coffee corner`
- `Works as a display-worthy brew object`

怎么写更自然：

- 用 `setup`、`countertop`、`coffee corner` 这类美国家居和咖啡器具语境词。
- 可以强调它适合什么场景，但不要暗示性能结果。

不要怎么写：

- `improves flavor`
- `precise extraction`
- `competition-ready`
- `barista-level control` unless verified

### 4. Specs

应该怎么写：

- `Catalog-listed specs`
- `Listed dimensions`
- `Pending sample review`

推荐格式：

- `Capacity: 500 cc (catalog-listed, pending sample review)`
- `Height: 15.6 cm (catalog-listed, pending sample review)`

不要怎么写：

- 裸写一串数字而不标状态
- `final specs`
- `measured dimensions` unless actually measured

### 5. Trust layer

应该怎么写：

- `Product preview`
- `Notify-only`
- `Details will be updated after sample review`
- `Pricing and availability are not live yet`

怎么写更自然：

- 用短句直说，不要道歉式长段落。
- 语气要像明确规则，不像客服解释。

不要怎么写：

- `coming soon` if timing is unknown
- `launching shortly`
- `shipping soon`
- `almost ready`

### 6. FAQ

FAQ 建议统一用这个结构：

- First sentence: direct answer
- Second sentence: boundary or clarification

示例：

- `Can I buy it now? Not yet. This page is a product preview and the current live action is product updates only.`
- `Is this the silver-lined version? No. The current preview is for the non-silver-lined version.`
- `Are these final specs? Not yet. Current dimensions are catalog-listed and pending sample review.`

## 直译陷阱与推荐替换

| 不建议英文 | 问题 | 推荐替换 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `purple copper` | 美式商品页不自然，像直译 | `copper` / `copper body` |
| `arrival notice` | 电商语境生硬 | `Notify Me` / `Get Product Updates` |
| `not silver inner wall` | 语法别扭 | `non-silver-lined version` |
| `craft feeling` | 空泛 | `bottle-shaped silhouette` / `textural copper finish` only if visible |
| `data preview` | 产品页不自然 | `product preview` |
| `manual hand pour kettle` | 冗余 | `pour-over kettle` |
| `waiting for sample verification` | 过于中式说明文 | `pending sample review` |

## 明确不能出现的词和承诺

### 禁止写的交易承诺

- price
- discount
- in stock
- ready to ship
- lead time
- preorder
- reservation
- waitlist priority
- early access

### 禁止写的检测 / 合规结论

- food safe
- food-grade
- tested
- certified
- compliant
- lead-free
- heavy metal safe

### 禁止写的性能承诺

- precise flow control
- flavor improvement
- cleaner cup
- stable extraction
- professional-grade
- competition-level

### 禁止写的未验证工艺 / 产地叙事

- handmade
- hand-forged
- artisan-made
- heritage craft
- traditional master technique
- origin story

### 不建议写的风格化词

- oriental
- zen
- ancient
- luxury
- collector's piece
- masterpiece

这些词的问题不是“永远不能用”，而是当前阶段没有证据支撑，且会把页面带向假奢侈或假文化叙事。

## 挂到首页和 PDP 时的最小执行规则

### 首页

- 首屏只保留 1 个主 CTA。
- Headline 说产品，subheadline 说边界。
- 不在 Hero 里塞研究过程、团队动机或采购背景。

### PDP

- 标题区先说 object identity，再说 preview status。
- 规格区每一条都带 `catalog-listed` 或 `pending sample review`。
- FAQ 只解决误解，不写品牌散文。

### 邮件收集区

- 按钮优先 `Notify Me`。
- 表单说明必须出现 `updates only`。
- 明确不是 `preorder`、不是 `reservation`、不是 `purchase commitment`。

## 推荐的当前挂载组合

如果要现在就给美国市场版首页一套最稳妥的英文首屏，建议直接用下面这组：

- One-line positioning: `Thoughtful copper objects, introduced one piece at a time.`
- Hero headline: `One Copper Kettle, Shown With Clear Boundaries`
- Hero subheadline: `This preview focuses on the non-silver-lined version and catalog-listed specs while sample details are still under review.`
- Hero body: `This page is a product preview for the non-silver-lined version. Pricing, availability, and sample-verified details will only be added after review.`
- Primary CTA: `Notify Me`
- Secondary CTA: `See Product Details`
- CTA microcopy: `For product updates only. No reservation or purchase commitment.`
