## 一、统一回复红线

客服、邮件和私域回复必须统一使用以下边界：

- 现在不能购买；当前页面不是购买页，只用于资料预览和到货通知。
- 不能预订、预约、候补、排队，也不能用提交邮箱换取优先购买资格。
- 当前锁定为非银内壁版本，不使用银内壁、镀银内壁、999 银内壁或 `silver-lined` 卖点。
- 规格只能说“资料显示 / catalog-listed”，正式发布前仍需样品复核。
- 留邮箱只用于到货通知和相关公开更新，不代表购买资格、锁库存或锁价格。
- 不承诺价格、库存、到货时间、开售时间、检测结论、控流安排、配送方案或售后结果。

## 三、邮件回复模板

### 3.1 首封确认邮件

Subject: `We received your product update request`

```markdown
Hi,

We received your request for updates about the copper bottle-shaped pour-over kettle.

This product is still in a preview-only stage. Checkout, pricing, inventory, delivery details, and final sample-verified information are not live yet.

Your email sign-up is only for arrival notifications and related public updates. It does not create a preorder, reservation, waitlist position, priority purchase qualification, inventory hold, or price lock.

If there is a verified public update for this item, we will share it by email.
```

### 3.2 客户追问开售时间

Subject: `About the current product preview status`

```markdown
Hi,

There is no confirmed launch date, arrival date, price, or inventory quantity for this item yet.

The page is currently a product preview and email update sign-up only. We will send a public update if the product status changes after sample review and business confirmation.
```

### 3.3 客户误以为已经排队成功

Subject: `Clarifying your product update sign-up`

```markdown
Hi,

Thanks for signing up for updates. To clarify, this sign-up does not create a preorder, reservation, waitlist position, purchase qualification, inventory hold, or price lock.

It only means we can email you if there is a public update about this product.
```

## 四、内部改写规则

若客服需要自行改写，必须保留以下句意：

- `product preview only`
- `not a purchase page`
- `no preorder, reservation, waitlist, queue, priority access, inventory hold, or price lock`
- `non-silver-lined version`
- `catalog-listed specs, pending sample review`
- `email is only for arrival notification and public updates`

不得出现以下表达：

- `reserve yours`
- `join the waitlist`
- `preorder now`
- `claim your spot`
- `early access`
- `stock is reserved`
- `price is locked`
- `launching soon`
- `shipping soon`
- `certified safe`
- `silver-lined interior`

## 六、发布前客服自检

发送前逐条确认：

- 是否明确说明现在不能购买，且不是购买页。
- 是否避免承诺预订、预约、候补、排队或优先资格。
- 是否没有使用银内壁相关卖点。
- 是否把规格标为资料显示并说明待样品复核。
- 是否说明邮箱只用于到货通知和公开更新。
- 是否没有承诺价格、库存、交期、检测、控流、配送或售后结果。
